## 💡 Pro Tips for Outreach

1. **Timing:** Send emails 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (peak open times)
2. **Personalization:** Use actual name, company, reference
3. **A/B Test:** Send 50 each of 2 different subject lines
4. **Follow-up:** After 3 days if no response
5. **LinkedIn First:** Connect before sending email
6. **Free Trial:** Always offer no-risk access
7. **Social Proof:** Once you have 1 customer, mention it!
8. **Urgency:** "Beta access, limited spots"
9. **CTA:** Make it clear what's the next step
10. **Mobile:** Optimize everything for mobile (most people check on phone)
